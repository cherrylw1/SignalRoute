Pipeline leaks are the silent killers of sales targets. 

Sales reps are busy. They manage dozens of accounts simultaneously, which inevitably leads to follow-up delays and slipping close dates. While CRM reports show deal values, they rarely tell you which deals are actively dying.

Enter **Scout AI**, your 24/7 automated sales manager.

## How Scout AI Protects Your Revenue

Scout AI works in the background of your HubSpot pipeline, analyzing three main variables:
1. **Activity history**: How long has it been since the last outbound email, call, or meeting?
2. **Engagement signs**: Is the client clicking proposal links or visiting your pricing page?
3. **Closing metrics**: How realistic is the close date relative to historic cycles?

### Deal Scoring (RED, AMBER, GREEN)

Scout scores every deal into three priority bands:
- <span style="color: #ef4444; font-weight: bold;">RED</span>: Immediate risk. Long inactivity or slipped dates. Requires urgent intervention.
- <span style="color: #f59e0b; font-weight: bold;">AMBER</span>: Slowing down. Needs a gentle nudge or a personalized value asset.
- <span style="color: #10b981; font-weight: bold;">GREEN</span>: Nominally healthy. On track to close.

### Next-Step Nudges

Instead of showing dashboards, Scout emails your reps with precise actions:
> *"Marcus, your deal with Velox hasn't replied in 6 days. They just viewed the security page. Email them this security case study: [Generate Draft]."*

By automating risk detection and providing pre-written, context-aware templates, Scout AI turns passive CRM records into active pipeline velocity.
