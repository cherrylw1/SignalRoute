Welcome to the future of inbound routing. 

At Churnaut, we're building the revenue personalization infrastructure for modern B2B sales and marketing teams. Our platform connects your CRM directly to your website. This ensures that every prospect clicking a tracked link lands on a page tailored precisely to who they are, what stage they're at, and what they need to see next.

## Why Deterministic Personalization?

Traditional personalization platforms rely on loose deanonymization signals like IP addresses. These yield low match rates and introduce latency. Churnaut takes a different approach:
- **Known identity instead of probabilistic matching**: We map a valid incoming routing token to its associated Churnaut session and CRM context.
- **A lightweight resolve path**: Our snippet is designed to apply approved personalization rules without rebuilding the customer website.
- **A narrower, more respectful boundary**: Personalization starts with a known link or configured signal rather than anonymous IP deanonymization.
